## Hex Game

![](/images/projects-hex-game.png)

https://ayeung.dev/hex-game/

HEX is a two-player connection game. Each player tries to create an unbroken chain of their stones connecting their two opposite sides of the board. This implementation is for two human players, and is best played on a tablet or a computer.

## 100 Cities for Kids

![](/images/projects-100-cities.png)

https://ayeung.dev/100-cities-for-kids/

A website introducing 100 cities in the world to kids. It introduces each city by talking about where it is, its people, language, food and culture, as well as famous people and fun facts about the city.


## Hexaflexagon Generator

![](/images/projects-hexaflexagon.png)

https://hexaflexagon-generator-517239745630.europe-west1.run.app/

A web app allowing you to upload three images and generate a hexaflexagon template that you can print out and fold up your own hexaflexagon.
